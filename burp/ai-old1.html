What is Burp AI?

Burp AI provides a secure, trusted platform for natively incorporating AI-powered functionality into Burp Suite.

The power of Burp AI, at your fingertips

Helping you uncover vulnerabilities faster, cut through repetitive tasks, and focus on the creative, high-impact testing that relies on your human intuition and expertise.

Simplify your existing workflow

Burp AI fits seamlessly into your existing workflow—augmenting your skills as a manual tester without taking control away from you. No convoluted workarounds required.

Burp AI, designed for security professionals

Designed to enhance your workflow and help elevate your time-to-value. Burp AI has been built on the foundations of decades of PortSwigger Research.

Secure, trusted, in your control

Burp AI delivers real-time, AI-powered testing—secure, private, and built for pentesters who demand trust. No data retention. No model training. Just smarter security. Read more about Burp AI's data security here.
