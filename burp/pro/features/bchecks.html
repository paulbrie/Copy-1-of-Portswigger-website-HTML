What are BChecks?

BChecks offer a powerful and efficient way to customize scan checks for Burp Scanner. Either download from the BChecks library or create them using a simple and easy-to-learn, domain-specific language.

Find vulnerabilities others can't

Use a BCheck to respond quickly to newly identified threats by creating and running a highly specific scan check to detect a particular CVE.

The power of the community

Explore the growing library of community-created BChecks to see how others are enhancing their security testing workflows.
EXPLORE BCHECKS LIBRARY

Where can I access BChecks?

BChecks are available in both Burp Suite Professional and Burp Suite Enterprise Edition. Write your own custom BChecks - tailored specifically to your own application - in Burp Suite Professional, or select a community-made BCheck from the BCheck library, and import them directly into Burp Suite Enterprise Edition.

BChecks in Burp Suite Professional

You can create, test, and apply BChecks in Burp Suite Professional.

Extend the range of vulnerabilities that Burp Scanner looks for without the hassle of building an extension using the Java-based Montoya API.

Once loaded within the BChecks tab, you can run custom scan checks that are highly tailored to your specific use case.
Learn more about BChecks in Burp Suite Professional

BChecks in Burp Suite Enterprise Edition

You can upload BChecks into the BChecks tab of the Extensions section and enable them on a site/folder level to be applied to any scans that target that site.

Any vulnerabilities identified from those BChecks will be displayed in the Issues tab alongside any other issues enabled by your scan configuration.
